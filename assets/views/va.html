About Us

A Leading, Family-Owned Cleaning & Virtual Assistance Provider

DelaCruzPH is a family-owned company serving businesses with after-hours commercial cleaning and reliable virtual assistance. We are fully insured, bonded, and covered by WCB—so safety, accountability, and professionalism come standard.

Beyond spotless facilities, we help teams run smoother through Virtual Assistant services: inbox and calendar management, documentation, scheduling, research, customer support, and more—tailored to your tools and SOPs.

We combine trained staff, clear SLAs, and weekly reporting. Expect responsive communication, measurable outcomes, and proactive improvements—not just task completion.

Whether you need daily/weekly cleaning or ongoing admin support, our flexible plans scale with your workload. With modern methods, eco-friendly products, and efficient digital workflows, we keep your space and operations clean, organized, and productive.
